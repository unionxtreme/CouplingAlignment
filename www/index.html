Coupling Align Pro

A coupling is the connection of two shafts for the purpose of transmitting power. Typical coupled-shaft arrangements include Motors and Pumps, gearboxes, fans, compressors & turbines. Coupling alignment refers to achieving a perfect rotational centreline between the two of more shafts that are coupled together, in order for the shafts to transmit power efficiently.
